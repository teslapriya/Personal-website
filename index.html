About me

My name is Priya Shukla. I am currently working on the deployment of "Galactic Radio Explorer" (GReX) in the USA, Australia, and India. The GReX is a low-cost radio telescope for searching microseconds galactic Fast Radio Bursts (FRBs). As an undergraduate student, I have explored the domain of astronomical instrumentation through various cool projects, including my work at Caltech on GReX and Square Kilometer Array Radio Telescope. I am passionate about exploring the universe by integrating engineering and astronomy.
